About Us

Objectives:

UK Chapter of the Alumni Association will act as a forum for all the ex-students of Jadavpur University to preserve the rich cultural heritage of the alma-mater and to work on charitable social causes in-line with the moto of the university and the parent organization. The association is a non-political and non-religious organisation dedicated to the welfare of the association members, ex-students and associates of the university and society at large.
